================================================================================
KEY FINDINGS FOR PATENT
================================================================================

CTAC improves V2I PDR by +6-7% over DCC baseline:
  Baseline (DCC): 87.64% overall V2I PDR
  CTAC K=2:       94.07% overall V2I PDR (+6.44%)
  CTAC K=4:       93.28% overall V2I PDR (+5.65%)

Why CTAC improves V2I PDR:
  - Reduces channel congestion (fewer simultaneous transmitters)
  - Lower interference at PHY layer
  - Fewer collisions means more packets reach RSU successfully
  - 121/118 vehicles achieve 100% V2I PDR with CTAC vs 0 with baseline

Combined V2V + V2I improvement:
  V2V Mean PDR: Baseline 0.9093 → CTAC K=2 0.9486 (+4.3%)
  V2I Overall:  Baseline 87.64% → CTAC K=2 94.07% (+6.4%)
  CTAC improves BOTH V2V and V2I reliability simultaneously
